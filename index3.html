MEMORY CARD GAME

About the Game

Flip-and-match memory games are fun and engaging activities designed to test and improve memory skills. Players typically start with a set of face-down cards, each featuring a pair of matching images. The objective is to flip over two cards at a time to find matching pairs. If a match is found, the player keeps the cards and continues their turn; if not, the cards are flipped back over. The game continues until all pairs are matched, and the player with the most pairs wins.!

Game Rules

1] Flip two cards to find a match.
2] If the cards match, you keep the pair and take another turn.
3] If they do not match, flip them back over after showing their faces, and the next player takes their turn.
4] The game continues until all pairs have been matched and collected.
